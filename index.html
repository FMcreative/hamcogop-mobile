About Us

OUR VISION STATEMENT:

We are accountable to our God and to all mankind to live out the Great Commandment (Matt.22:37-40) and to fulfill the Great Commission (Matt.28:19-20).

OUR MISSION STATEMENT:

Reaching up to God. Reaching out to the world and Reaching in to one another.

Doctrine

As a Church, We believe:

  • That there is one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • In the deity of Christ, in His virgin birth, in His sinless life in the physical miracles He performed.
  • In His atoning death on the cross, in His bodily resurrection, in His ascension to the right hand of the Father.
  • In His personal return in power and glory at His second coming.
  • In the ultimate unity of all believers as prayed by Christ in Jn. 17.
  • In the sanctity of human life and the sanctity of the marriage bond and the importance of strong, loving godly families.

Ministries

Sunday School/ Christian Ed.

The Sunday School/Christian ed. Caters to everyone regardless of age. Please join us every Sunday morning at 10am to 11am for dynamic life-changing spirit-filled bible studies with classes for all ages. We also offer an annual vacation bible school and an annual community picnic. Free bus ride is available on Sunday morning, if you desire ride, please call 905-523-8065 in advance.

Youth Ministries

Our youth ministry offers vibrant life-changing programs for youths from age 13-35. Every Friday evening from 7:30 pm to 9:30 pm our youth are engaging in bible studies, relevant life discussions, music, socials and games. The forth Sunday morning of each month the youths are in charge of service.

Children's Ministries

This ministry is one of our most important ministries because they are very young and tender and they are our future leaders. In our children's ministry we have a children's church going on at the same time as the adult church. This service is prepared and served at their age level according to the wise man's admonition "train a child in the way in the way he should go, and when he is old he will not turn from it." (Prov.22:6).

Ladies Ministries

The Ladies Ministry is a very vital ministry in the church, while this ministry ministers specifically to ladies it is multi-faucet it raise mission funds and also help to train the young ladies according to Titus2: admonitions. Every second Sunday evening you can join the ladies ministry in their ladies focus service. This ministry offers relevant bible studies, issues relevant to women along with their group prayer.

Mens Ministries

Mens Ministry is very necessary and needed for the times we are living in, according to Titus 2: "The older men must teach or transmit godly values to the young men." Every third Sunday evening the Men's Ministry conduct their services which are quite relevant to men, this includes discussions (men's issues) and there is also monthly informal get-together.

Seniors Ministries

The Seniors Ministry ministers to and honours those who have laboured and are in the twilight of their years. They are full of wisdom and have so much to share. Every second Friday of the month our senior's meet for their monthly tea party and fellowship.
